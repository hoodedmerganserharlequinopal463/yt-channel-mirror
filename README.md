## 📁 What is this app?

This application allows you to save entire YouTube channels to your computer. You keep a copy of your favorite videos. You do not need an internet connection to watch them once they exist on your hard drive. This tool works locally on your machine. Your data remains private. You control the files.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

- Windows 10 or Windows 11
- 500 MB of free hard drive space
- A stable internet connection for the download process
- A folder where you store your video library

## ⚙️ Setting up the software

Run the installer once the download finishes. Follow the simple steps on your screen.

1. Double-click the file you downloaded. 
2. A window appears. Select Run if your computer asks for permission to open the file.
3. Follow the prompts in the installer window.
4. Choose a destination folder on your computer.
5. Click Install.

The software creates a shortcut on your desktop. You use this icon to start the program later.

## 🎞️ Saving your first channel

You define which videos to save by using the channel URL. 

1. Open the application from your desktop.
2. Go to the YouTube website in your web browser. 
3. Open the channel page you want to save.
4. Copy the web address from the top bar of your browser.
5. Paste this address into the text box inside the application.
6. Click the button labeled Start Mirror.

The application checks the channel for new videos. It begins the download process. You see a progress bar for each video. Do not close the window while the bar moves. The files save to the folder you chose during setup.

## 📂 Managing your library

The app organizes files by the channel name. Each video includes the title and the upload date. You can open these files with any standard media player. Windows Media Player, VLC, or similar programs work fine. You can move these folders to an external hard drive if your internal disk runs low on space.

## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app delete videos from the internet?**
No. The app only creates a copy on your computer. The original video stays on the YouTube website.

**Can I stop the download in the middle?**
Yes. You can pause the process. You can resume it later when you have more time or better internet speeds.

**How much space do videos take?**
High-definition video files can be large. A channel with many videos uses several gigabytes of space. Check your available storage before you start a large download.

**Can I update the channel later?**
Yes. You can press the update button at any time. The app compares your local folder with the YouTube channel. It only downloads the new videos you do not have yet.

**Does this work for private videos?**
No. This tool only saves public content. You must be able to view the video in your web browser for the tool to copy it.

## 🛡️ Troubleshooting common issues

**The app does not start.**
Restart your computer. Check if your antivirus software blocked the program. You may need to add the program to your list of trusted applications.

**Download speeds are slow.**
This depends on your internet service provider. You can limit the number of simultaneous downloads in the settings menu to free up your bandwidth for other tasks.

**I cannot find my videos.**
Click the Open Folder button inside the main menu of the tool. This jumps directly to the location where the app stores your files.

**The app shows an error during download.**
This often happens if you lose your internet connection. Wait for your connection to return. The tool will retry automatically after a few minutes.
